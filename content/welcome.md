## Contest
Here is where all the contests and problems to practice show.
In each contest you will see 2 lines with blue text:

- Enter: See all problems in the contest, if contest isn't end, this lines is a green button with white text
- Virtual participation: You want to participate contest like you are participate is on time, click it. This line only show after contest end.

## Problem/ Contest page

### 1. Problem
Here will show all problem in contest, after you choose one of these problem, you can see the statement of the problem you choose to solve

### 2. Submit Code
Here you will submit your code to judge. This page has 4 parts:

- Problem: here you choose the problem you want to submit your solution(code)
- Language: here you will choose the compiler you want (GNU G++ is for C++). You only need to choose your compiler on the 1st time you submit, after that the judge will select the nearest change of your language is the main language you use to submit
- Source code: Here you will copy all your code and paste here to send to the judge
- Or choose file: Here you click on "choose file" button to upload your solution file (your code file). If you use this please leave the source code box blank, else please don't click to upload any files.
After complete click on the submit button to send your solution (code) and the page will negative you to status page.

### 3. My submission
Here will show your solution (code) status when judging (or after judged)

### 4. Status
Like the my submission page but here will show all solution status from all members in contest

### 5. Standing
Your rank in the contest

### 6. Custom Inovation
Small IDE for you to test with the judge
